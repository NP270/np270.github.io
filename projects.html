Noxidus

Noxsidus is an exciting and immersive annual event organized by the Bandaranayake College Union of Astronomy, aimed at inspiring students by sparking their curiosity and passion for the wonders of the universe. This night camp is designed to offer an unforgettable experience, filled with a series of lectures, workshops, astronomy-related activities, and celestial observations

Forarex

Forarex is the annual Intra-school water rocket workshop and competition organized by BCUOA. It brings together enthusiastic young minds to explore science through creativity and teamwork. Participants learns the physics behind rocket design, build their own models, and launch them in a spirited contest, making the event both educational and exciting.

Constant

Constant, annual Astronomical day organized by BCUOA is a prominent event in the Sri Lankan school astronomy calendar, offering a full day dedicated to science, creativity, and the wonders of the universe. At its heart lies the prestigious Solomon Dias Bandaranayake Challenge Trophy, Inter-School Astronomy Quiz Competition, drawing top teams from leading schools island-wide.

NOXSIDUS ’25

Noxsidus ‘25 was an exciting and immersive event organized by Bandaranayake College Union of Astronomy in April 2025. This night camp was designed to offer an unforgettable experience, filled with a series of lectures, workshops, astronomy-related activities, and celestial observations. The primary objective of Noxsidus was to foster a deeper understanding of astronomy, allowing students to explore the vastness of space and the science behind the stars, planets, and galaxies., The event has managed to ignite the imaginations of young minds and encourage them to pursue a lifelong interest in science and astronomy.

CONSTANT '24

The 8th edition of "Soloman Dias Bandaranayake Challenge Trophy" took place on 13th of September 2024. The top 25 teams from the preliminary round advanced to the finals held in Bandaranayake College premises. After a theoretical round, a spot test and a data analysis round, Mahamaya Girls College emerged as the champions.
