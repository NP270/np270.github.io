Bandaranayake College
Union Of Astronomy

Founded in 1996, the Union of Astronomy of Bandaranayake College has inspired celestial exploration for nearly three decades. Registered with the Arthur C. Clarke Institute in 1997, it quickly became a leader in astronomical education, hosting its first night sky observation camp in the same year. Driven by curiosity and wonder, the union continues to explore the limitless possibilities beyond our world.
